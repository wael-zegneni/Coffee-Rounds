How It Works

A quick guide to the coffee round system.

The Basics

Every Thursday, the group goes for coffee. One person pays for everyone that week. The app tracks who paid, who was present, and how much was spent — so everything stays fair over time.

Rotation

There's a fixed rotation order that determines who pays next. After someone pays, the pointer moves to the next person in the list.

Example: The order is Nick → Pooya → Sankar → Wael. Nick pays this week, so next week the pointer moves to Pooya.

Absence Rules

If it's your turn but you're absent, you still owe one round when you come back. The app skips to the next available person for that week, but the pointer stays on you until you pay.

Example: It's Pooya's turn but he's on holiday. The app asks if Sankar can pay instead. Next week when Pooya is back, it's still his turn — he owes one round.

Being absent when it's not your turn has no effect on the rotation. You simply don't appear in that week's "present" list.

Logging a Round

Go to the Log tab. Pick the date, select who paid, check who was present, enter the total amount, and hit "Log Round." The data syncs instantly to all devices.

Example: On April 17, Nick paid €18.50 for 5 people. Select April 17, tap Nick as payer, uncheck the 2 absent people, type 18.50, and log it.

Scoreboard

The Board tab shows each person's stats: how many times they paid and how many times they were present. The ring chart shows paid count at a glance.

Managing the Team

Go to Settings to add or remove people. New members are added to the end of the rotation. When someone is removed, their historical data is preserved — they'll still appear in past rounds and stats.

Manual Pointer Override

If the rotation gets out of sync (e.g. after entering historical data), go to Settings and tap the ▶ button next to any person to set them as the next payer.
